# Nyx 1.3.0 — 2026-07-11

Music and wallpaper polish: lyrics rendered onto the wallpaper, smooth song
transitions, a live download queue, and genre-based smart playlists.

## Added
- **Lyrics on the wallpaper** — synced lyrics from LRCLIB drawn behind the cards,
  following the browser player line-by-line. Settings: on/off, text size,
  animation (fade / slide / glow / blur), position, color (white or softened
  accent), custom **font upload** (`.ttf/.otf/.woff`), and a **sync offset**
  slider for sped-up / remixed tracks.
- **Crossfade** — smooth blend between songs via an A/B audio pair; slider in
  settings → playback (0–12s); works on next/prev, queue, and auto-advance.
- **Download queue** — a panel listing every active/recent download with live
  progress bars, done/failed states, and "clear done".
- **Smart playlists** — auto-grouped chips from your library's genres (plus
  "shuffle all"); one click shuffles that group into the player.
