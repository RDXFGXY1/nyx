# Nyx 1.1.0 — 2026-07-08

## Added
- **Cross-tab audio control** — the Media pane (Alt+D → Media) shows a **"playing
  in your tabs"** section listing every browser tab producing sound. For each tab:
  **play / pause**, **mute / unmute** (the mute button lights up in your accent),
  and **click the title to jump to that tab**. The list refreshes live and hides
  when nothing is playing. Uses the already-granted `tabs` permission — no new
  permissions.
- **Weather popup** — clicking the temperature opens a glass card with city,
  condition emoji, big temperature, "feels like", wind, humidity, last-updated
  time, and a live **Refresh** button.

## Fixed
- **14" / high-DPI laptop layout** ("everything too big / clipped") — on smaller
  effective viewports (a 14" laptop at 125–175% Windows scaling) the fixed-size UI
  was oversized and the clock/weather were clipped off the right edge. The UI now
  scales down proportionally with tiered `zoom` breakpoints; fixed full-screen
  backgrounds/overlays still cover correctly.
- **Card drag "ghost" showed a broken orange half-outline** — the browser was
  snapshotting the card with its hover accent border. Border/shadow/transform are
  now neutralized the instant the drag starts, so the dragged card looks clean.
- **Hard black shadow on cards + visible column scrollbar** — the card drop-shadow
  was clipped into a hard black edge by the scrolling columns, and the scrollbar
  covered content. Cards are now flat glass and column scrollbars are hidden while
  still scrolling.
